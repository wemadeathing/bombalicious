Growing Social Following Fresh Daily Since July 2024

Since opening our doors in July 2024, Bombalicious Bakes has quickly become Surrey Estate's most beloved neighborhood bakery. What started as a dream to bring quality traditional baking to our community has grown into a local gathering spot where neighbors become friends.

We bake fresh every morning, combining time-honored South African recipes with contemporary quality and unmatched value. From our popular hot cross buns to our traditional custard slices, every item is crafted with love and priced to bring joy to your everyday.
